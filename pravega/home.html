Pravega return to the innovative grounds of the Indian Institute of Science. We bring to you spectacular cultural events, mind-bending Sci-Tech events and stellar musical performances that everyone can jam to. As it turns 10, we continue to rejuvenate Pravega every year with exciting new events. This year, we brought to you Carvaan, a flagship project where we went to some of India’s biggest cities for offline auditions to cultural events. Come be a part of the Pravega family, grab some cool souvenirs, and dive headfirst into the awesomeness! Join our celebrations, where science, culture, passion, and music converge in all their glory.
